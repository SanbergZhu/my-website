LONGXIN located in Wenling, Zhejiang province, was established in 2017. The company founder had been working with welding machines since 2005. With rich experiences in management and innovative ideas in market demands, he finally set up LONGXIN MACHIENRY with a great team.
As a young player in welding machine field, our team have been attaching great importance in machines ’development and quality control from the first day of the company ’s registration. 
As we have been mainly focused on foreign market until now, it ’s clearly known that poor quality products will cause big trouble to our clients and consequently bring failure in our company growth.
